FAQS

What is the price and what services are included in the rate?

The cost varies based on room type and availability. All reservations include a bed, access to common areas such as the kitchen, and the opportunity to participate in our day and night activities.

What type of rooms do you offer and how many beds are in each room?

We provide a range of room choices, including a Twin and Twin Bunk Bed, 3 Bed Mixed Dorm Ensuite, 4 Bed Mixed Dorm Ensuite, 6 Bed Mixed Dorm Ensuite, 9 Bed Mixed Dorm and 12 Bed Mixed Dorm Ensuite.

Are there private rooms available or only shared dormitories?

We offer a variety of room options, including Twin and Twin with bunk beds.

What are the cancellation and refund policies?

Standard Rate: After the booking is made, we will charge the 1st night as a deposit. This deposit is fully refundable, given that you cancel 2 full days before your scheduled check-in. Failure to cancel within this time will result in a cancellation charge equal to the first night of your stay. If you do not show up for your reservation we may charge the first night of the accommodation to your credit card. Non-Refundable Rate: If your booking is non refundable the full amount will be charged in advance. If you choose to cancel your booking, or in the case of a no-show, you will be charged the full cost of your booking. Cancellations are only accepted via e-mail and must be received at least 2 full days before arrival. If you have made a “non-refundable” reservation on OnefamHostels.com, Hostelworld or Booking.com, you won’t be entitled to a refund if you cancel.

What is the exact location of the hostel?

📍 Sarphatipark 58I, 1073 Amsterdam

Do you have shuttle services to and from the station or airport?

We do not.

Are there lockers or lockers available to store my belongings safely?

Yes! We have lockers available in every room and complimentary luggage storage.

Can I use the kitchen to prepare my own meals?

We have a basic kitchen for preparing simple meals or snacks.

Are there common areas where guests can socialize and meet other travelers?

Yes!

Is free wifi available throughout the hostel?

Yes, we offer free high-speed wifi for our guests.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check in: 3:30pm / Check out 11:00am

Are sheets and towels provided, or should I bring my own?

Bed sheets are included and we offer towels for rent.

Do you have information on local tours and activities that can be booked from the hostel?

We offer city tours and activities that you can join for free. You can also reach out to our knowledgeable staff for local recommendations and travel tips.

Is there a curfew?

Nope! Feel free to drop by the hostel whenever you like.

Are there any age-specific or membership discounts?

We provide exclusive discounts for guests staying at Onefam Hostels across multiple cities. go to our Onefam Community section on our website header to join.

How to get to the hostel from the Airport?

Take the train from Schiphol Airport to Amsterdam Zuid, then transfer to Metro line 52 and get off at De Pijp station—Sarphatipark 58I is a short walk from there.

Top 5 tourist hotspots near Onefam Amstel Hostel Amsterdam.

Rijksmuseum.

Immerse yourself in Dutch art and history, including masterpieces by Rembrandt and Vermeer.

  • How to get there: From the hostel, walk about 5 minutes to the tram stop “Sarphatipark”. Take tram line 3 towards Station Muiderpoort or tram line 12 towards Station Sloterdijk and get off at the stop “Rijksmuseum”. The journey takes approximately 5-10 minutes.

Van Gogh Museum.

Explore the world of the iconic painter Vincent van Gogh.

  • How to get there: From the “Rijksmuseum” tram stop, it’s just a short 5-minute walk to the Van Gogh Museum.

Anne Frank House.

A poignant and important historical site where Anne Frank and her family hid during World War II. (Book your tickets well in advance!)

  • How to get there: From the hostel, walk to the tram stop “Sarphatipark”. Take tram line 12 towards Station Sloterdijk and get off at the stop “Westermarkt”. From there, it’s about a 10-minute walk to the Anne Frank House. The total journey takes approximately 20-25 minutes.

Vondelpark.

Amsterdam’s largest park, perfect for a relaxing stroll, a picnic, or just soaking up the local atmosphere.

  • How to get there: From the hostel, it’s a pleasant 10-15 minute walk to the entrance of Vondelpark. Alternatively, you can take tram line 3 from “Sarphatipark” towards Station Muiderpoort for one stop and get off at “Van Baerlestraat”, which is right by the park.

Heineken Experience.

Discover the history of one of the world’s most famous beers in this interactive brewery tour.

  • How to get there: From the hostel, it’s just a short 5-minute walk to the Heineken Experience, which is located very close to Onefam Amstel Hostel Amsterdam.
