Single rooms

Offering air-conditioned accommodation with free Wi-Fi and LCD satellite TV, the family run Hostel Sonce is a 5-minute walk from the historic centre of Ptuj. Free private parking is provided. Built in 1927, the building was completely renovated in 2011.

The accommodation units at Sonce are bright and elegantly decorated and feature hardwood floors and a heating system, as well as views of Ptuj Castle or the Old Town. Each room has a private bathroom.

Guests can use a computer and watch TV in the common area, which is located on the ground floor. Sonce has a small garden with different plants and a seating area.

There is a vending machine on site with a selection of drinks and snacks. A  grocery shops, bars and restaurants start 100 m from the hostel. Ptuj Castle and the Old Town are 500 m away.

Terme Ptuj Spa Resort and Ptuj Golf Club are 1 km from Sonce Hostel. Main Bus and Train Station are 2 km away. Maribor Airport is at a distance of 25 km.
